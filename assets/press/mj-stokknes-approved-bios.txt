M. J. STOKKNES — APPROVED PUBLIC BIOS

SHORT BIO
M. J. Stokknes is a Norwegian author and independent publisher based in Andalucía, writing philosophical nonfiction, speculative fiction, dystopia, and bureaucratic noir.

MEDIUM BIO
M. J. Stokknes is a Norwegian author based in Andalucía, Spain. His work moves between philosophical nonfiction and fiction, examining power, perception, identity, information, belief, time, intimacy, institutional pressure, and the systems people learn to mistake for reality. He writes the ten-domain Chaos & You series and publishes through Stokknes Publishing House.

EXTENDED BIO
Born in Haugesund, Norway, and based in inland Andalucía, M. J. Stokknes writes across philosophy, systems, speculative fiction, dystopia, and bureaucratic noir. The nonfiction examines recurring pressure structures—civilization, entropy, faith, power, identity, information, love, philosophy, time, and reality—while the fiction places similar machinery inside workplaces, housing, debt, technology, bodies, roads, and ordinary relationships. The books are produced through an independent author-led publishing operation.

PUBLIC REFERENCE
Use M. J. Stokknes or Stokknes in editorial copy. Use Marius Johan Stokknes where full bibliographic or legal identification is required.

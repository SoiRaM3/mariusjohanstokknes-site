EDITORIAL POSITION

A real independent operation. Not a fictional corporation.

The house is deliberately small, author-led, catalogue-first, and honest about its scale.

01

Books first

The operation exists to produce, protect, revise, and distribute the work—not to make the machinery look larger than it is.

02

Edition truth

Live, draft, unavailable, translated, and in-development formats are presented as they actually exist.

03

Human standards

Clear metadata, readable interiors, honest review-copy terms, controlled public claims, and no automated imitation of institutional importance.

WHAT THE HOUSE CONTROLS

From unstable document to public edition.

The private system is not offered as a product and the public does not need access to it. Its job is to stop manuscripts, editions, metadata, and release claims from quietly becoming different realities.

OPERATING PRINCIPLE

An edition is described as it actually exists.

Published is not draft. Translated is not available. A retailer listing is not the same thing as a controlled current edition.

CURRENT SCOPE

One author. A controlled catalogue.

Stokknes Publishing House is currently the author-led imprint for the work of M. J. Stokknes. It does not present itself as a multi-author trade publisher and is not accepting unsolicited manuscripts.

THE PRIVATE LAYER

The machine is not the work.

Manuscripts, continuity, editions, proofing, launch status, assets, and catalogue records are maintained through a private local publishing system. The public site receives only what has deliberately crossed the boundary.

The author

M. J. Stokknes remains the public front door. The house is the operational layer beneath the books.

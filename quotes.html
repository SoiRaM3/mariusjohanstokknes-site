07 · Hermetic Notes

Small sentences for larger rooms.

These fragments are deliberately stripped of context. They are not doctrine. They are pressure points.

The map becomes dangerous when it forgets that it is a map.
The mirror becomes a prison when recognition replaces intimacy.
The system survives because its pressure arrives as convenience.
Every structure becomes mythology once people forget the conditions that produced it.
Certainty is often what exhaustion calls relief.
A culture can know everything and still become unable to understand itself.
The machine does not need belief. Repetition is enough.
The body remains the final court of appeal.
The archive remembers more than the human being and understands less.
Chaos is not the absence of structure. It is structure without your permission.

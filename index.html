Marius Johan Stokknes

Marius Johan Stokknes, writing as M. J. Stokknes
Author of the Chaos Series and the Stokknes Chaos Framework.

Writing on power, perception, identity, information, faith, entropy, civilization, and the systems people mistake for reality.

The Chaos Series is a connected body of philosophical work examining how human beings construct reality through incomplete information, emotional bias, institutional pressure, and systems of reinforcement.

This work does not attempt to comfort what it exposes.

Marius Johan Stokknes

Author of the Chaos series

Writing on power, perception, and the systems people mistake for reality.
This work does not offer solutions.

The Work

Entropy, Chaos & You
On disorder, mental fragmentation, and decay

Core Thesis

The work explores how individuals construct reality through incomplete information, emotional bias, and external systems of control. It does not attempt to resolve these tensions, but to expose them.

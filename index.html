Civilization, Chaos & You

“This book will not fix civilization.”

Civilization is not a masterpiece.

It is a frightened animal wearing infrastructure.

Built from roads, borders, laws, markets, institutions, algorithms, inherited wounds, and billions of nervous systems attempting to survive one another, civilization likes to present itself as rational.

It is not.

Civilization, Chaos & You dissects the machinery beneath ordinary life: the panic beneath bureaucracy, the mythology beneath nations, the appetite beneath markets, the metric behind the person, and the algorithm learning the rhythm of the hand holding the screen.

The Stokknes Chaos Theory Compendium

A mythic-analytical framework for understanding human collapse.

The framework begins with a simple structural problem: human beings do not interact with reality directly. They interact with interpretations shaped by memory, emotion, information, identity, and external systems of reinforcement.

Under modern conditions, these systems can collapse faster than meaning can stabilize.

Information → Overload → Anxiety → Simplification → Certainty

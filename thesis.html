The Chaos Framework

By Marius Johan Stokknes

The work of Marius Johan Stokknes is based on a recurring structural pattern: individuals do not interact with reality directly. They interact with interpretations shaped by memory, emotion, and external systems of reinforcement.

Across domains — identity, time, power, and information — the same structure appears:

  • Perception is incomplete
  • Interpretation fills the gaps
  • Systems reinforce the interpretation
  • The individual mistakes the result for reality

This creates a closed loop in which:

  • Illusion stabilizes itself
  • Contradictions are absorbed rather than resolved
  • Understanding becomes performance rather than clarity

The Chaos series does not attempt to resolve this structure. It isolates it, traces it, and makes it visible.

Domains

The framework operates across multiple domains:

  • Reality
  • Time
  • Identity
  • Information
  • Love
  • Philosophy
  • Entropy
  • Civilization
  • Faith
  • Power

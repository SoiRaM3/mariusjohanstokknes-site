The books form a connected body of work examining perception, power, identity, information, and the systems people mistake for reality.

Civilization, Chaos & You
On institutions, collapse, order, spectacle, and collective dependency.
Faith, Chaos & You
On belief systems, meaning, doubt, surrender, and metaphysical survival.
